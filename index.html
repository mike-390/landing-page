Some random information

The Espresso is a bold and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans,
creating a rich and intense flavor that serves as the base for many coffee drinks.
The Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam,
offering a rich and creamy flavor perfect for any time of the day.
Black Coffee is a pure and simple brew made from ground coffee beans and hot water, offering a bold, robust
flavor without the addition of milk or sugar, perfect for those who appreciate the true essence of coffee.
Americano Coffee is a smooth and mild coffee made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, creating a
lighter flavor while maintaining the rich aroma of espresso, perfect for a longer and more relaxed coffee experience.
